What is a cryptoeconomy?

A cryptoeconomy is an ecosystem of transactions and services all conducted digitally via crypotcurrencies. The cryptoeconomy provides secure and high-speed solutions for all users while lowering the cost of transactions to a fraction of the price. A circulation of fund between buyers, sellers, consumers, and customers ensure that there is always a market for users to find their lifestyle solution. Due to the nature of the blockchain technology, cryptoeconomies are decentralized, making them secure and almost impossible to be hacked. All blockchain transactions are publicly available, which makes them safe and reliable.

What is     blockchain?

A blockchain is a growing list of records that act as a ledger on a decentralized database. Transactions are combined into “blocks” and added onto the previous “block” periodically. These “blocks” are then decoded for verification by volunteers from all over the world, with a small reward, called miners. This creates a trusted and accurate ledger system that is very difficult to hack or manipulate. Once a block has been verified by miners, it is placed back into the blockchain adding onto the list of previous transactions. All the blocks are permanently recorded and stored in every single miner’s database, therefore able to constantly cross verify discrepancies.
